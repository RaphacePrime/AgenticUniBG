# International Students pre-evaluation online

If you are Non-EU student and have a Bachelor Degree obtained outside Italy, then your educational qualification must be verified by the University of Bergamo before enrollment in the chosen [**Master’s Degree program taught in Italian or English Language**](/node/7454).

Please be informed that, according to the new regulation, with reference to the **Bachelors achieved in** [**India**](https://www.cimea.it/Upload/Documenti/3245_6456_india_cis-it-IT.pdf) **and** [**Pakistan**](https://www.cimea.it/Upload/Documenti/3256_6467_pakistan_cis-it-IT.pdf), the general Bachelor does not give access to a Master Degree course. To access to a Master Degree course, a **Honours Bachelor** is needed.

[Masters' degrees taught in English complete list](#)

European and Non-EU prospective students wishing to enroll at one of the following Master Degree Programme taught in English (Academic Year 2022-23)  
can request a preliminary online evaluation of their qualification.

[MA](https://ls-eadap.unibg.it/en)[Accounting, Governance and Sustainability](https://ls-ags.unibg.it/en "Return to the Accounting, Governance and Sustainability home page")  (free access)

[MA Clinical Psychology for Individuals, Families and Organizations](https://ls-pc.unibg.it/en) (limited enrollment program)

[MA Economics and Data Analysis](https://ls-egm.unibg.it/en) (free access)

[MA Intercultural studies in languages and literatures](https://ls-llepa.unibg.it/en)(free access)

[MA International management and marketing](https://ls-imef.unibg.it/en)  (free access)

[MA Management Engineering](https://ls-ig.unibg.it/en) (free access)

[MA Mechatronics and](https://ls-mec.unibg.it/en) [Smart Technology Engineering](https://ls-mec.unibg.it/en "Return to the Mechatronics and Smart Technology Engineering  home page") (free access)

[MA Planning and Management of Tourism Systems](https://ls-pgst.unibg.it/en) (free access)

[MA Economics & Finance](https://ls-ef.unibg.it/en) (free access)

[MA Medical Engineering](https://ls-mede.unibg.it/en) (free access)

[MA Philosophical Knowledge: Foundations, Methods, Applications](https://ls-pfma.unibg.it/en) (free access)

Pre evaluation calls

**First call - Free access MA**

* **From November 24th 2025 to January 15th 2026**: prospective students can apply
* **February, March 2026**: online admission interview
* **Within March 31st 2026**: ranking publication (eligible students will receive a notification by e-mail)

**Second Call – Free access MA and limited access MA**

* **From January 1st 2026 to January 31st 2026**:prospective students can apply
* **March , April 2026**:online admission interview
* **Within April 30th 2026**: ranking publication (eligible students will receive a notification by e-mail)

How to apply

1. Register on our website  
   Please see our  [**registration guide**](https://en.unibg.it/sites/sten/files/avvisi_e_comunicazioni/registration_of_students_internet_desk.pdf)
2. Pay the 30 Euro pre-evaluation fee  
   Please see our payment [**procedure guide**](https://en.unibg.it/sites/sten/files/internazionalizzazione/2_procedura_pagamento_concorso.pdf)
3. Apply to the online pre-evaluation through the [**system DreamApply**](https://apply.unibg.it/)(up to two applications)  
   You have to perform a new registration process in this system.  
   **Compulsory documents (see below attached checklist)** to be uploaded on the application (if any of these 5 documents will not be submitted, your application will be rejected):  
   **Copy of the Bachelor degree**  
   **Copy of the official Academic transcript or Diploma supplement**  
   **English certificate**  
   **Copy of your valid passport**  
   **MAJOR AREA FORM duly filled in (please find the form below)**

# International Students pre-evaluation online

If you are Non-EU student and have a Bachelor Degree obtained outside Italy, then your educational qualification must be verified by the University of Bergamo before enrollment in the chosen [**Master’s Degree program taught in Italian or English Language**](/node/7454).

Please be informed that, according to the new regulation, with reference to the **Bachelors achieved in** [**India**](https://www.cimea.it/Upload/Documenti/3245_6456_india_cis-it-IT.pdf) **and** [**Pakistan**](https://www.cimea.it/Upload/Documenti/3256_6467_pakistan_cis-it-IT.pdf), the general Bachelor does not give access to a Master Degree course. To access to a Master Degree course, a **Honours Bachelor** is needed.

[Masters' degrees taught in English complete list](#)

European and Non-EU prospective students wishing to enroll at one of the following Master Degree Programme taught in English (Academic Year 2022-23)  
can request a preliminary online evaluation of their qualification.

[MA](https://ls-eadap.unibg.it/en)[Accounting, Governance and Sustainability](https://ls-ags.unibg.it/en "Return to the Accounting, Governance and Sustainability home page")  (free access)

[MA Clinical Psychology for Individuals, Families and Organizations](https://ls-pc.unibg.it/en) (limited enrollment program)

[MA Economics and Data Analysis](https://ls-egm.unibg.it/en) (free access)

[MA Intercultural studies in languages and literatures](https://ls-llepa.unibg.it/en)(free access)

[MA International management and marketing](https://ls-imef.unibg.it/en)  (free access)

[MA Management Engineering](https://ls-ig.unibg.it/en) (free access)

[MA Planning and Management of Tourism Systems](https://ls-pgst.unibg.it/en) (free access)

Pre evaluation calls

**First call - Free access MA**

* **From November 24th 2025 to January 15th 2026**: prospective students can apply
* **February, March 2026**: online admission interview
* **Within March 31st 2026**: ranking publication (eligible students will receive a notification by e-mail)

**Second Call – Free access MA and limited access MA**

* **From January 1st 2026 to January 31st 2026**:prospective students can apply
* **March , April 2026**:online admission interview
* **Within April 30th 2026**: ranking publication (eligible students will receive a notification by e-mail)

How to apply

1. Register on our website  
   Please see our  [**registration guide**](https://en.unibg.it/sites/sten/files/avvisi_e_comunicazioni/registration_of_students_internet_desk.pdf)
2. Pay the 30 Euro pre-evaluation fee  
   Please see our payment [**procedure guide**](https://en.unibg.it/sites/sten/files/internazionalizzazione/2_procedura_pagamento_concorso.pdf)
3. Apply to the online pre-evaluation through the [**system DreamApply**](https://apply.unibg.it/)(up to two applications)  
   You have to perform a new registration process in this system.  
   **Compulsory documents (see below attached checklist)** to be uploaded on the application (if any of these 5 documents will not be submitted, your application will be rejected):  
   **Copy of the Bachelor degree**  
   **Copy of the official Academic transcript or Diploma supplement**  
   **English certificate**  
   **Copy of your valid passport**  
   **MAJOR AREA FORM duly filled in (please find the form below)**

1. ACCURATEZZA: non rimuovere MAI date, scadenze, URL o dati specifici. Sono informazioni critiche.
2. STRUTTURA: quando la risposta elenca più elementi o passi, usa sempre un elenco puntato/numerato.
3. CONCISIONE INTELLIGENTE: elimina solo frasi vuote, ripetizioni e formule di cortesia inutili. NON tagliare contenuto informativo.
4. NIENTE DISPERSIONE: no introduzioni del tipo "Ottima domanda", no conclusioni ridondanti.
5. FONTI: se la risposta contiene una "Pagina di riferimento" con URL, MANTIENILA sempre in fondo.

REGOLE DI FORMATO (testo semplice, compatibile con tag <p> HTML):
- NON usare markdown (no **, *, #, ecc.)
- Per elenchi usa "- " oppure "1. " con un elemento per riga
- Per enfasi usa MAIUSCOLE
- Niente emoji o simboli decorativi
- La risposta deve iniziare direttamente con il contenuto, senza formule di apertura

LUNGHEZZA TARGET:
- Risposta semplice (un fatto, un'informazione): 1-3 frasi
- Risposta articolata (procedura, elenco con scadenze): max 10-15 punti brevi
- Se la risposta contiene scadenze o date multiple, NON comprimerle: sono tutte importanti

PROCESSO DI REVISIONE:
- Se la risposta è già concisa e corretta, restituiscila invariata
- Se è troppo lunga, elimina le parti ridondanti mantenendo TUTTI i dati fattuali
- Se è un paragrafo con più elementi, convertila in elenco puntato
- Correggi eventuali errori grammaticali
- Verifica che non siano stati inventati dati non presenti nella risposta originale

Rispondi SOLO con la risposta rivista, senza commenti aggiuntivi.
